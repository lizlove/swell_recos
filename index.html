Takeaways

Our advice on what to spend

A stroller is one of those items that you are going to be using every day, therefore we recommend paying a few extra bucks for a stroller that has more features, you can skimp on other things that will be used less frequently.

What to consider

  • Maneuverability is key, it is recommended to test drive your stroller with a 25 pound weight in it to see how it handles. On the same note, larger wheels make the stroller easier to maneuver.
  • Make sure your stroller has a car seat adapter, this makes the transition from car to stroller that much easier.
  • Your stroller seat should fully recline to lie flat or have a bassinet included so that your infant can sleep safely.
  • Don’t forget to think about you! Make sure your stroller has plentiful room for all the of the things that you need to take with you.
